OURB

A City Building Network

OURB BRINGS CITIZENS, PLANNERS, INVESTORS, AND CITY OFFICIALS TOGETHER, IN ORDER TO INSPIRE A COLLECTIVE DEVELOPMENT OF A CITY.

Based on an interactive map

Highlight crucial areas of your city and consult with neighbors about ideas, resources, and challenges.

Sketch ideas, leave comments, upload proposals

Vote on the proposals as a neighborhood and bring them to the attention of investors and city officials.
