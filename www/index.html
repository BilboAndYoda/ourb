OURB

A City Building Network

OUR/B BRINGS CITIZENS, PLANNERS, INVESTORS, AND CITY OFFICIALS TOGETHER, IN ORDER TO INSPIRE A COLLECTIVE DEVELOPMENT OF A CITY.

Based on an interactive map

Highlight crucial areas of your city and then consult with neighbors about ideas, resources, and challenges.

Sketch your ideas, leave comments, and upload your proposals

Vote on the proposals as a neighborhood and bring them to the attention of investors and city officials. All from your phone or laptop.
